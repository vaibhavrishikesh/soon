Privacy Policy — Soon

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Soon ("the app") is a countdown app. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data we collect ourselves

None. The countdowns you create (titles, dates, icons, colors and reminders) are stored only on your device. We do not have a server, we do not have accounts, and your countdowns are never sent to us.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Soon is free and is supported by ads served through Google AdMob. To show ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect and process:

  • Device and advertising identifiers (e.g. the IDFA)
  • Advertising and usage data (such as ad interactions)
  • Approximate (coarse) location, where available
  • Diagnostics, such as crash and performance data

This data is used to deliver and measure ads. On first launch the app asks for your permission to track (Apple's App Tracking Transparency). If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be less personalized.

Google's handling of this data is governed by their own policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy. You can learn how Google uses data from apps at https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Photos / Notifications

The app may ask to send you reminder notifications for your countdowns. These are scheduled locally on your device. You can turn them off any time in iOS Settings.

Children

Soon is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
